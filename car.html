Chevrolet Camaro

The 2024 Chevrolet Camaro ZL1 is a high-performance sports car that combines aggressive styling with a powerful engine and advanced technology. It features a supercharged 6.2-liter V8 engine that produces 650 horsepower and 650 lb-ft of torque, paired with either a six-speed manual or ten-speed automatic transmission. The ZL1 is equipped with performance enhancements such as Magnetic Ride Control, an electronic limited-slip differential, and Brembo brakes for superior handling and stopping power.
